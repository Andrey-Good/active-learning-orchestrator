# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants are expected to keep discussion professional, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- giving clear technical feedback
- assuming good intent while still being precise
- documenting decisions and tradeoffs
- disagreeing with evidence and without personal attacks

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or personal insults
- discriminatory language
- publishing private information without consent
- derailing technical discussions with hostile behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.

If you need to report a concern, contact the repository maintainers privately.
